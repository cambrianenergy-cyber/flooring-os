## Billing Model

### Company-Wide Pricing (Not Per-Seat)

**Why not per-seat?**

Per-seat creates chaos:
- "Hire 1 person = add $99/month" (hidden cost)
- "Don't add that contractor" (cost block)
- "Disable licenses when not in use" (friction)

**Company-wide pricing:**
- âœ… CFO understands cost immediately
- âœ… Easy to budget ("$299/month, everyone uses it")
- âœ… No hidden per-user surprises
- âœ… Scales naturally ("grew to 15 users? Upgrade tier once")

### Billing Cycles

- **Monthly:** Standard, full price
- **Annual:** Save ~5% (pay upfront for year, 1 month free)
- **Enterprise:** Custom contract (quarterly, net-30, etc.)

### Free Trial

- **7 days:** Full Square Core access
- **No credit card required** (builds goodwill)
- **Upgradable mid-trial** (try Scale features for $X)

## Pricing Psychology

### Why This Works

1. **No one feels "cheap"**
   - Lowest tier is $299/month = serious business filter
   - Filters out "just testing" users
   - Attracts professionals who know software costs

2. **Price grows with confidence, not fear**
   - $299 (Core) = "Can I trust this?"
   - $699 (Operations) = "Yes, and we're growing"
   - $1,299 (Scale) = "This is our system"
   - $2,500+ (Enterprise) = "This is infrastructure"

3. **CFO-friendly**
   - Single line item on budget (not 15 per-seat charges)
   - Scales predictably (grow users, upgrade tier once)
   - Easy contract negotiation (Enterprise known upfront)

4. **Prevents abuse**
   - No $99/month per-user race-to-bottom
   - No "unlimited users for $299" exploit
   - Extra user packs are controlled ($199 per 5)
